Meet The Team

About The Team

The People Behind Your Financial Transformation

Matthew Spenillo

CEO | Personal Finance Coach

Professional Background

Matthew brings a unique combination of real-world financial experience and professional licensing to his coaching practice. He is a licensed health insurance agent in the state of Florida, giving him a grounded understanding of the financial services landscape and the regulatory environment that protects consumers.

Before launching Spenillo Financial Wellness, Matthew spent years in the workforce navigating the same financial challenges most Americans face — managing a household on a single income, balancing debt obligations, and trying to build long-term wealth without a financial background or family guidance to lean on.

The Story Behind The Mission

Matthew didn't grow up learning about money. By the time he and his wife started building a life together, they were drowning in consumer debt, fielding constant collection calls, and seriously considering bankruptcy as their only option.

Instead of giving up, he got obsessed. He spent years self-educating — budgeting systems, debt payoff strategies, investing fundamentals, retirement planning, negotiation tactics with creditors. He took on every single debt collector himself. No lawyer, no professional help. Just discipline, strategy, and relentless follow-through.

Today, they own a home, drive a new car, hold retirement accounts and investment portfolios, carry zero consumer debt, and know exactly where every dollar goes. That transformation wasn't handed to them — it was built from the ground up.

Why He Coaches

Matthew started Spenillo Financial Wellness because he knows what it feels like to stare at a bank account and feel hopeless — and he knows what it feels like to come out the other side. His coaching is strictly educational, focused on budgeting, debt management, savings habits, and financial literacy. He doesn't provide investment advice, and if a situation requires a licensed professional, he'll always be transparent about that.

If I can do it, you can too. Let me show you how.
